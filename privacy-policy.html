PlanarNest – Privacy Policy
Last updated: 2026-01-29

This Privacy Policy explains how PlanarNest (“we”, “us”) collects and uses information when you use our website and desktop application (“PlanarNest”).

1) Information we collect

a) Purchase & licensing information (via Lemon Squeezy)

When you purchase PlanarNest, our payment provider Lemon Squeezy processes your payment and provides us with information such as:

  • purchase email
  • order/transaction details
  • license/activation status

We do not store your full payment card details.

b) Support communications

If you contact us at contact@planarnest.com, we receive the information you provide (e.g., email address, message content, attachments).

c) Diagnostic reports (Case Reports / logs)

PlanarNest may generate diagnostic files to help improve stability and fix bugs. These may include:

  • application version / OS information
  • settings relevant to the job
  • geometry needed to reproduce a bug (e.g., paths), depending on what you export

Upload behavior

  • By default, case reports are not uploaded automatically.
  • If you enable Auto-send in settings, PlanarNest may upload case reports to the configured endpoint.

2) How we use information

We use the information to:

  • provide licensing and access to the software
  • process refunds and resolve purchase issues
  • respond to support requests
  • diagnose bugs and improve the software (when reports are provided or uploaded)

3) Sharing

We share information only with:

  • Lemon Squeezy (payment processing / licensing)
  • service providers strictly necessary to operate the product (if any)

We do not sell your personal information.

4) Data retention

We retain information only as long as needed for:

  • licensing and transaction records
  • support and troubleshooting
  • legal/accounting obligations

5) Your choices

  • You can choose whether to export/share diagnostic files.
  • You can keep Auto-send off (default) to avoid automatic uploads.

6) Contact

Questions about this policy: contact@planarnest.com
