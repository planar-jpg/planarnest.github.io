PlanarNest – Refund Policy
Last updated: 2026-01-29

Refund window

Purchases are eligible for a refund within 14 days of the purchase date.

How to request a refund

Email contact@planarnest.com with:

  • order number (or receipt)
  • purchase email
  • reason (if it’s a bug, include steps to reproduce and any relevant logs/screenshots)

What happens on refund

If a refund is granted, your license/activation may be revoked and access to the Software may be disabled.

Abuse / limitations

We may refuse refunds in cases of clear abuse, including repeated refund requests, chargeback abuse, or other fraudulent activity.

Chargebacks

Please contact us first before filing a chargeback so we can help resolve issues quickly.
